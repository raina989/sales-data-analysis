## Project Overview
This project analyzes sales data from a sample superstore to uncover insights about sales trends, top products, regional performance, and the impact of discounts. 
The goal is to provide actionable insights for business decisions.

## Objectives
- Analyze sales trends across regions and product categories
- Identify top-selling products
- Examine seasonal or monthly trends in sales
- Understand the effect of discounts on sales performance

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

## Key Insights
- The sales distribution is right-skewed: most orders are small, with a few large orders contributing heavily to revenue.
- Top 20 products account for a significant portion of total sales.
- Certain regions outperform others, suggesting a focus area for marketing and inventory.
- Discounts impact sales differently: moderate discounts increase sales, while extreme discounts show mixed results.
